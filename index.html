How to Make

Vanilla pudding


This recipe will give you instructions on how to whip up a simple Vanilla Pudding recipe from scratch.

Utensils required are

  • 3L Pot
  • 4 Cup Measuring Cup
  • Set of measuring spoons
  • Whisk
  • Ramikens

Any bowl works, Ramikens just look nice.

For this recipe you will need

  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons Cornstarch
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • 3 Whole Eggs
  • 2 1/2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1 TeaSpoon Vanilla Extract

    • If you use salted butter, you can reduce or entirely skip the salt at the beginning.

Video demonstration at bottom

  1. In the pot, mix the sugar, cornstarch and salt.
  2. In the measuring cup, measure the amount of milk required.
  3. Crack the eggs into the milk and mix.
  4. Turn on the stove to medium high heat and place the pot on it.
  5. Pour the milk and egg mixture into the pot.
  6. Stir until all the dry and wet ingredients are blended.
  7. Continue to stir until the mixture becomes thickened.
  8. Take off heat.
  9. Add butter and vanilla extract. Stir.
  10. Pour into the ramekins and serve.

Variants of this pudding recipe you can do to mix things up
Chocolate Pudding:
Add a couple of chunks of chocolate while the vanilla pudding is hot
Let it melt, then stir it in until pudding is chocolate coloured.
Fruit Desert:
Layer the pudding into a cup with some fresh chopped fruit.
Feel free to add whatever you wish! Fruit, Nuts, Sprinkles, any topping.
Pudding Cake:
This can be made two ways.
Simply pour some pudding onto a piece of cake to make it extra special.
OR pipe the pudding into the middle of the cake.
